## Competitive Alternatives Considered

### Why NOT Per-User Pricing?
- Industrial crews are large (15-30 people)
- Per-user pricing penalizes large teams
- Creates friction ("Do we really need to add this welder to the system?")
- Per-project aligns better with how budgets are allocated

### Why NOT Usage-Based (Per Weld)?
- Hard to predict costs (creates buyer anxiety)
- Penalizes active users (opposite of what we want)
- Complex pricing page (harder to communicate value)

### Why NOT Freemium Forever?
- Industrial contractors have budgets ($500K-$10M projects)
- Free tier attracts wrong customers (hobbyists, tire-kickers)
- 14-day trial is sufficient to prove value
- Want to monetize from day one
