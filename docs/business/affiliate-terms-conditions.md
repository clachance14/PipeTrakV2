## 3. Commission Structure

### 3.1 Standard Commission Schedule

**Tier 1 (Casual Referrers):**
- Years 1-2: 30% of Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Years 3-5: 15% of MRR
- Year 6+: 10% of MRR

**Tier 2 (Active Referrers - 5+ customers):**
- Years 1-2: 35% of MRR
- Years 3-5: 20% of MRR
- Year 6+: 15% of MRR
- $500 bonus per customer referred

**Tier 3 (Partners - 10+ customers):**
- Option A: 1-2% equity in PipeTrak LLC
- Option B: Cash commission (40% → 25% → 20%)
- $1,000 bonus per customer referred

### 3.2 Commission Calculation

- Commissions calculated on **collected revenue only** (not invoiced revenue)
- Based on customer's monthly subscription amount
- Excludes taxes, processing fees, chargebacks, and refunds
- Adjusts automatically if customer adds/removes projects
- Starts after customer's first paid invoice (trial period excluded)

### 3.3 Qualifying Referral

A referral qualifies for commission if:
- Customer is new to PipeTrak (not an existing user or trial)
- Customer was properly attributed to you via referral link, code, or manual attribution
- Customer completes signup and makes first payment
- Customer was not already in our sales pipeline prior to your referral

### 3.4 Attribution

**First-touch attribution:** The first Affiliate to refer a prospect receives credit.

**Exceptions:**
- If prospect requested demo or started trial before your referral, no credit
- If prospect was directly contacted by our sales team before your referral, no credit
- If multiple Affiliates claim same referral, we review and decide based on timestamps

### 3.5 Changes to Commission Structure

- We reserve the right to modify commission rates for **new referrals**
- Existing referrals (prior to change) are grandfathered at original rates
- Changes announced 30 days in advance
- Continued participation after changes constitutes acceptance
